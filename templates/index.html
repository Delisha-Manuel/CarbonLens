CarbonLens: Know, Reduce, Repeat

Find out your carbon footprint, and take steps to reduce it!



The current global average carbon footprint is approximately 4.7 metric tons (tCO2) per person annually, and in high-consumption regions like the United States, the average footprint is significantly higher, at approximately 14.2 to 16 metric tons per person! To meet global temperature goals, experts say that the average global footprint needs to drop below 2 to 2.5 tons per year!

With CarbonLens, you can help reach this goal. Simply input your stats below, and the random forest regressor will estimate your carbon footprint, and the LLM will suggest steps to help you reduce it.
